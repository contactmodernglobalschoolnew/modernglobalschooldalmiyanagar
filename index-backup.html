Our Story

About Modern Global School

Modern Global School (MGS) is a private educational institution established in 2019 in Dalmianagar, Rohtas district of Bihar. The school provides quality education from Grade 1 to Grade 8 along with a pre-primary section.

The school follows Hindi as the medium of instruction and aims to provide a balanced learning environment that focuses on both academic excellence and overall personality development.

Located in an urban area and easily accessible by all-weather roads, the school has been serving the educational needs of the local community for many years.
